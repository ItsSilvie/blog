This card is part of the Dawn of Ashes First Edition set.

Effect This card costs 2 less to activate if your champion has dealt attack damage this turn. Until end of turn, target Sword weapon gets +3 and "On Champion Hit: Draw a card."
Rules 2022-12-27 – On Hit only triggers when 1 or more damage is dealt from the source. If all of the damage was prevented, then On Hit will not trigger.
